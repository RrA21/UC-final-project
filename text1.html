Drawing By coffee

Coffee paint is created simply by adding coffee (usually instant) to water and varying the strength to get different tones. Some even boil the coffee to get the darkest colors possible. From that point all you need is a bit of talent and some watercolor paper to create your own aromatic works of art.





Steps of How drawing by using coffee:

  1. Tools:
  2. Watercolour Paper
  3. Pencil and eraser
  4. Coffee powder
  5. Mixing plate
  6. Brushes
  7. Scrap paper
  8. Masking tape
  9. Paper napkin
  10. Clean water
  11. Rag
